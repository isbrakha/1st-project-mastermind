HOW TO PLAY

• Your goal is to decipher the secret code which consists of a series of 4 colored pegs

• Click the colored pegs to place them in the table, you want to duplicate the exact colors and positions of the secret code

• You can't place pegs of the same color in the same guess row, the secret code is made of 4 different colors

• After each guess you will get feedback in the form of black and white pegs, black pegs represent correct position and color, white pegs represent correct colo in wrong position!

• Guess the code in 8 turns or less to win, see how many times you can win in a row!
